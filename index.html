About BlueMark.

BlueMark Real Estates specializes in all types of properties across Dubai.

Property Management

BlueMark provides management services, In terms of advertising the property, selecting credible tenants, signing tenancy agreements, collecting rents and maintaining properties through in-house or outsourced maintenance teams, complying with local rules and regulations, and reporting to Owners with regard to their properties.

Snagging & Handover

At BlueMark. We help clients to take the handover of their new property from the developer, even if they are not in Dubai. We also provide snagging services before handover. A detailed inspection, like electrical, water heating, ventilation, air conditioning, fire safety, bathroom equipment, leakage, laundry room systems, etc.

Selling & Leasing

BlueMark offers the owners unparalleled customer service, whether selling or leasing. A dedicated executive will assist you throughout the process while offering you all the benefits of our leasing, selling, and management services. We will find quality tenants for you, on short or long- term rentals, to ensure the maximum.
